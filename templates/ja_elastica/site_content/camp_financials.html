Camp Dues

All dues are 'due' by August 15th. We do NOT accept dues on the playa.

Options for your level of participation and associated dues:

  1. Working Member: $100
    I will be available to help for 2 days from arrival day (regardless of arrival day), sign up and show up for (1) 4.5-5 hour Hosting Shift and Strike* on Sunday from either 10am-5pm or 12-7pm. Includes a separate $500 collateral deposit check which will be torn up if you indeed honor your Strike* commitment. However, if you don't show up at your agreed upon Strike shift, we will keep the deposit in full. If you don't want to help our camp with Strike*, you'll want to sign up as a Supporting Member (see below).
    * International Members: Your Strike deposit can be paid via PayPal for $100USD. We guarantee a full refund, in cash and on-playa, immediately after your Strike commitments are met. We promise!
  2. Supporting Member 1: $275 (Early Bird Tier)
    I am an Opulent supporter with no setup or Strike responsibilities, but still a cool and conscientious camp member and will be thoughtful, nice and responsible to those around me. (this limited lower tier for Supporting Member are on a first paid, first accepted basis)
  3. Supporting Member 2: $350 (Late Comer Tier)
    I am an Opulent supporter with no setup or Strike responsibilities, but still a cool and conscientious camp member and will be thoughtful, nice and responsible to those around me. (this limited lower tier for Supporting Member are on a first paid, first accepted basis)

Power Fees:

  • $100 per RV - plug into the camp power grid your own generator isn't used. (RV's only)
  • $300 Tour Buses
  • $50 for tents using an AC unit or other heavy power draw.
  • $20 for tents using minimal power for lights, re-charging phones, cameras, etc.

For more information on power see Plugging Into Camp Power on Camp Overview page.
